About Me


I am Ben-Zheng Li (李本正), a PhD candidate (advised by Prof. Xi-Le Zhao) with the School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China. My Ph.D. research project is "Modeling and Algorithm for High-dimensional Data Processing". My research interests include (but are not limited to) high-dimensional data processing, tensor modeling, and efficient algorithms.
