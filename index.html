About Me


I am Ben-Zheng Li (李本正), a Ph.D. candidate (advised by Prof. Xi-Le Zhao) with the School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China. My Ph.D. research project is "Modeling and Algorithm for High-dimensional Image Processing". My research interests include (but are not limited to) computer vision, high-dimensional image processing, and tensor modeling and algorithm.
