About Me


I am Ben-Zheng Li (李本正), a Ph.D. candidate (advised by Prof. Xi-Le Zhao) with the School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China. My Ph.D. research project is "Modeling and Algorithm for High-dimensional Data Processing". My research interests include (but are not limited to) high-dimensional data processing, tensor modeling, and efficient algorithms.

Experiences


  • 09/2022-Current: University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC); Ph. D. student in Applied Mathematics (Supervisor: Prof. Xi-Le Zhao)

  • 09/2019-06/2022: University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC); Master student in Applied Mathematics (Supervisor: Prof. Xi-Le Zhao)

  • 09/2015-06/2019: East China University of Technology (ECUT); Bachelor student in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics (Supervisor: Prof. Guang Zeng)
